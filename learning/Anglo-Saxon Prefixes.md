A prefix is a sound placed **before** and connected to a word or root to form a new word.

- Why study prefixes?
  prefixes can enlarge vocabulary. You have a clue to the meaning of every word beginning with that prefix. 
  
--------------

fore - beforehand 미리~ , front 앞~ , before 이전~

(beforehand)
foreboding = misgiving, omen
forecast = prediction, prophecy
foreshadow = augur, portend
foregoing = going before, preceding, previous
forebear = ancester 

(front)
forearm 팔뚝
forefront = foremost, vanguard
foremost = principal, first, front, chief
foresight = prudence, 선견지명
foreword = preface, prologue, introduction

(wrongly)

misfire 불발탄
